Family Sunset at the Zoo

Enjoy a summer evening with the family at the Central Florida Zoo & Botanical Gardens.

On the fourth Friday of each month, we’ll reopen the gates at 5:30 p.m. and for just $5 admission per person, guests can enjoy exploring the Zoo at sunset, music, and our Splash Pad! Concessions by Sonny’s will be available for purchase.

We’ll reopen the gates at 5:30 p.m. exclusively for adults, over the age of 21.

Masks are required for guests 13 and older. All Zoo rules and modified operations apply. This event is not included for Annual Pass Holders; admission is $5 for all guests.

ABOUT OUR PARK

Manhattan’s Central Park Zoo is one of the oldest in the country, first opening on its current site in 1861. In the 1980s, the Wildlife Conservation Society assumed management of the park under a contract with the City of New York and completed a dramatic five year transformation. Since reopening in 1988, our mission has been to engage future generations of conservationists and create wildlife champions.
