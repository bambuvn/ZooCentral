Sunset at the Zoo, presented by GrayRobinson

An Adults-Only Evening at the Zoo!

Get wild at the Central Florida Zoo & Botanical Gardens’ Sunset at the Zoo, presented by GrayRobinson.

On the third Thursday of each month, we’ll reopen the gates at 5:30 p.m. and for just $5 admission, adults over the age of 21 can stroll through the Zoo enjoying live music, purchase some refreshing beer and wine, and see the animals during sunset!
