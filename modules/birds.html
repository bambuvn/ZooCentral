Take Flight with the Zoo’s Birds

You can probably identify birds by their feathers, but did you know that feathers are made up of keratin, the same substance that makes up our fingernails and hair?

Throughout the Zoo, you will see a huge variety of birds, including birds of prey, tropical parrots and even some local wildlife fluttering in the trees.
