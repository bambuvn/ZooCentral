SMALL WITH BIG CLAWS!

They are nocturnal, and like small, wet places to sleep. They are small, shiny and black with two pincer claws, called pedipalps.

About the Species

Many species

People often confuse black scorpions with Malaysian forest scorpions and while they are surprisingly similar, they are actually distinct and individual species.

Words From the Experts

These bugs are awesome because one of their most distinguishing features is how they glow under UV light!

Dave

Did You Know?

When they are threatened, Asian Forest Scorpions usually use their pedipalp (claws), which is capable of drawing blood, rather than their tail.
