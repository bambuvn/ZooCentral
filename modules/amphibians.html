Amphibians at the Zoo

At the Central Florida Zoo, you can see a variety of amphibians in our Herpetarium, including poison dart frogs, salamanders, and the African bullfrog. We also house amphibians in our Insect Zoo, inside the Wayne M. Densch Discovery Center. Two great places to cool off while visiting the Zoo!
