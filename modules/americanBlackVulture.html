BLACK ON BLACK!

These birds have dark black skin, covered in dark feathers. Though their legs appear white, they are truly black and only get their white appearance from the vulture urinating on itself to clean off after eating or to stay warm.

About the Species

Nature's Cleanup Crew!

These scavengers play an important role in cleaning up things like roadkill! They eat carrion, or dead animals, which is why they don't have feathers on their head!.

Did You Know?

Black Vultures lack a voice box and so their vocal abilities are limited to making raspy hisses and grunts.
